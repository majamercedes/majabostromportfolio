Neatbox

Neatbox is a project I did as my admissions day task as a part of getting in to Hyper Island.
I got in and during the fall we had our mobile landscape module with the task to design
something for the future mobile landscape. I wanted to develop this idea together with my
team and we came up with some more features.
The target users of neatbox are people who want to store their stuff in an easier way.
By using the app the users can find where their items are located and will also
be able to know if there is interest for the items on the second hand market.

Role: Design, Illustrations, Development, research, UX, UI

Tools: Illustrator, Photoshop, Agile-working, LOFi/HiFi prototype, Brainstorming, QR-tags,
Possible future of IBeacon stickers that can talk to each other.
