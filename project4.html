Pushed away

Pushed away is a project I did during six weeks in our development module at Hyper island.
The brief was to visualize a solution regarding the world crisis and my team choosed to
tackle the situation of refugees by using web code and tell a story about the journey that
refugees are going through when they leave their countries to find safety.

The process started off with ideation and Lo-Fi protoype on paper and when we felt that
everyone where on board we started to build the MVP/Hi-Fi with HTML and CSS to
discover parts to be clear out before getting to the finished product.
To collabroate in the team and share our code we used GitHub.
The response of the site was very positive and voted as the best solution in the class.
