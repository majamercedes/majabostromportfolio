Bumpaphone

I often find it hard to handle business cards. Most of them look similar and it's easy to loose them and
forget what I needed a persons business card for.
Bumpaphone is a business card in the form of an app with more possibilites to pick specific stuff that
you want to share with different contacts.

It works like this:
1. The user choose what he/she wants to share in the profile. This can be different for different contacts.
2. The user hold its phone close to the person that he/she wants to share business card with and the
information transfers over to the others app.
3. To remember when the persons met or why they have each others business cards it's possible to
either record a voice message or write a note about it.

Role: Design, Illustrations, research, UX, UI

Tools: Illustrator, Photoshop, Bluetooth, Brainstorming.
